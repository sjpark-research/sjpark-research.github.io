## Sang Jun Park

Researcher in heat transport, thermal metrology, thermoelectrics, and spin caloritronics.

## Research highlights

- 20 SCI(E) publications, including 9 first/corresponding-author papers
- First/corresponding-author publications in Physical Review Letters, Materials Today, Acta Materialia, and Matter
- Development of thermophysical imaging platforms for high-throughput discovery of transverse thermoelectric materials
- Expertise in thermal measurement, magneto-thermal transport, and spin–charge–heat coupling
