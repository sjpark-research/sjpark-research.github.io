Sang Jun Park

I study heat transport and thermal energy conversion in nanoscale, interfacial, and magnetic materials.

My research interests include quantitative thermal measurement, thermoelectric transport, spin caloritronics, and thermal materials/devices. I am particularly interested in developing reliable methods to measure and understand thermal transport in ultrathin films, interfaces, and device-relevant materials.

Research interests

  • Quantitative thermal measurement and thermal metrology
  • Heat transport in ultrathin films and interfaces
  • Spin and orbital caloritronics
  • Thermoelectric materials and transverse thermoelectric effects
  • Heat-flux sensors and thermal devices
  • High-throughput thermal-property measurement
